# Competitor Matrix — Dealer Rady

SMS / AI instant-response SaaS competitors for small BC used-car dealers.

---

## BDC.AI

**Source:** https://www.bdc.ai/
**Year:** 2026 (live site snapshot)
**Sample:** 250+ dealerships, 75,207 conversations handled
**Quote:** "The #1 Trusted AI BDC For Dealerships. Cut Response Times 10x with Customizable BDC.AI Sales & BDC.AI Service Agents That Voice Call, Text, and Email Leads Instantly. Set Appointments, Follow-Up, and Hot Transfer Qualified Calls To Your Team, All Day Long!"
**URL:** https://www.bdc.ai/

**Product:**
- BDC.AI Sales (voice/text/email lead engagement, 8s response, 2.7x more appointments, +40% show rate, 10x capacity)
- BDC.AI Service ($58K+ monthly revenue recovered, 95% book rate, 2x RO upsell)
- Receptionist AI (100% answer rate, <1s pickup, 24/7, 10+ languages)
- "InstantConnect" — click-to-call CTA on SRP/VDP pages

**Integrations:** CDK, Tekion, Cox Automotive, PBS Systems DMS; VinSolutions, eLead, DealerSocket, DriveCentric CRM; xTime, myKaarma, DealerFX service schedulers; vAuto, HomeNet, VINCUE, DX1 inventory; Dealer.com, DealerInspire, Overfuel, Team Velocity website providers; OfferLogix FTC-compliant pricing.

**Pain points called out (market data):**
- "23% of dealership calls go unanswered"
- "$120K+ lost in revenue per month"
- "67% annual BDC staff turnover"

**Pricing:** NOT PUBLIC — "Book a Demo" / "Talk to Your AI Agent" only. (Sales-led, enterprise-style pricing typical of BDC category — likely $1,500–$5,000+/mo/rooftop based on public reviews; needs further verification.)

---

## Conversica

**Source:** https://www.conversica.com/
**Year:** 2026 (live site snapshot)
**Sample:** 1.5 billion conversations served
**Quote:** "Conversica's AI Agents help you start effortless conversations that build trust, deepen relationships, and drive growth. ... Run 24/7 conversations across email, SMS, chat, and messaging apps in any language you need."
**URL:** https://www.conversica.com/

**Product:**
- AI Agents (not a single feature — full agent platform)
- Channels: email, SMS, chat, messaging apps, multilingual
- Use cases: Generate Demand & Acquire Customers / Deliver Exceptional Service / Keep Customers Coming Back
- Industries served: Sports & Entertainment, **Automotive**, Hospitality, Enterprise, Higher Education
- "Proven in 1.5 Billion Conversations"
- "Enterprise-Grade Security and Compliance: From GDPR and SOC 2 to data residency and encryption"

**Customer quote (Channel partner case study):** "The customer actually responded on Friday of Labor Day weekend, when everybody was out of the office. But our AIAgent was there. The rep got back into the office on Tuesday, picked up the phone, and responded to the customer live. And by Friday, we had a $500,000 deal." — John Hansen, Sr. Director, Field Marketing

**Pricing:** NOT PUBLIC. "Get Started" / "Schedule A Demo" only. (Enterprise-tier SaaS — historically $2,500–$10,000+/mo.)

---

## Podium

**Source:** https://www.podium.com/
**Year:** 2026 (live site snapshot)
**Sample:** 60,000+ businesses
**Quote:** "Turn more conversations into revenue with a 24/7 AI Employee that manages the full customer journey from first inquiry to booking and beyond. ... AI built for your industry. Designed to convert."
**URL:** https://www.podium.com/

**Product:**
- "AI Employee" — 24/7 lead response, appointment booking, database reactivation
- Industry verticals include **Auto Dealerships**, Home Services, Aesthetics, Retail
- Phone integration, two-way texting, payments, review requests (legacy Podium stack)
- Sales line visible: 1-801-438-4425 (Utah HQ)
- Conversion-focused messaging: "The #1 converting AI Employee for local businesses"

**Pricing:** NOT on homepage. Plans page exists (https://www.podium.com/plans — to be verified). Public reporting (G2/Capterra) suggests Starter ~$299/mo, Professional ~$449+/mo, with per-location add-ons and SMS usage fees.

---

## Kenect

**Source:** https://www.kenect.com/
**Year:** 2026 (live site snapshot)
**Sample:** dealer verticals include Automotive, Equipment, Powersports, RV, Marine, Outdoor Power
**Quote:** "The AI revenue engine for dealers. AI for dealerships. Conversations that drive revenue. ... Bring all your communications and data into one AI-powered platform where you can automate your systems for driving sales, improving your reputation, texting customers, and filling your service lanes."
**URL:** https://www.kenect.com/

**Product (dealer-specific):**
- Two-way business texting (text-enable existing business line)
- "Text Us" website widget (claims "260%+ increase" in qualified website leads)
- Reputation management / review generation ("3x reviews in first 90 days", "290+ 5-star reviews in 14 months" — West Central Equipment)
- Service lane texting (auto-notify when ready)
- Payments (text-to-pay)
- DMS/CRM integrations
- AI features (newer, less mature than BDC.AI / Conversica)

**Stated outcomes:**
- 260%+ increase in qualified leads from website
- 3x reviews in first 90 days
- 98% of text messages are read by customers
- 2.5 hrs saved per employee per day
- "Average Kenect customer increases revenue 6% in the first 12 months"

**Pricing:** NOT on homepage. Public reviews / G2 listings cite ~$300–$500/mo/location, with SMS usage and per-number fees on top. (To be verified via kenect.com/pricing or sales call.)

## Fullpath (Cox Automotive)
**Source:** https://www.fullpath.com/news/
**Year:** 2024 (Cox acquisition complete)
**Note:** Fullpath (formerly CDG, formerly Auto/Mate CDG) was acquired by Cox Automotive in 2024. Now sits alongside VinSolutions and Dealer.com in the Cox dealer-stack. Marketing platform focused on data/CRM/email — not a pure-play AI BDC.
**URL:** https://www.fullpath.com/


## VinSolutions (Cox Automotive)
**Source:** https://www.vinsolutions.com/
**Note:** Cox Automotive's flagship CRM for dealers. Has AI-driven lead-handling features bolted on. Sells to franchise dealers; the small BC used-car dealer is typically too small to be a VinSolutions customer (the CRM alone is $400-800+/mo/user).
**URL:** https://www.vinsolutions.com/


## PureCars / LotLinx (Lead-Handling Tools)
**Source:** https://lotlinx.com/about-us/
**Year:** 2024-2025
**Note:** LotLinx is a lead-handling / VIN-level marketing platform. Pricing NOT publicly listed. Not a direct AI BDC competitor — focuses on digital advertising and lead routing, not instant SMS/AI conversation.
**URL:** https://lotlinx.com/


## Gubagoo
**Source:** https://www.gubagoo.com/
**Year:** 2024-2025
**Note:** Live chat + SMS + digital retailing. Reynolds acquired Gubagoo (Reynolds & Reynolds is the DMS giant). Pricing NOT publicly listed. Capterra listings: https://www.capterra.ca/software/178750/chatsmart. Tends to sell to franchise dealer groups.
**URL:** https://www.gubagoo.com/
