## Cox Automotive 2024 Car Buyer Journey Study (CBJ)
**Source:** https://www.coxautoinc.com/insights/cox-automotives-car-buyer-journey-study-reveals-record-high-satisfaction-among-new-and-ev-buyers/
**Year:** 2024 (study), Jan 7, 2025 (release)
**Sample:** 15th annual CBJ study (large multi-year consumer panel)
**Quote:** "New-vehicle buyers reported a record 75% satisfaction rate with the overall shopping experience, the highest in the study's history."
**Quote:** "Buyers completing key steps online, such as applying for credit or pre-filling paperwork, saved an average of 42 minutes at the dealership, streamlining the process significantly. New-vehicle buyers saved more time – at 49 minutes – while used-vehicle buyers saved 40 minutes."
**Quote:** "Eighty-two percent of new-vehicle buyers who completed more than half of the required steps online expressed high satisfaction with their dealership experience."
**Quote:** "Satisfaction among all vehicle buyers, however, declined slightly in 2024 compared to the prior year... among both new and used buyers, 67% were highly satisfied with the journey, down from 69% in the previous study."
**URL:** https://www.coxautoinc.com/insights/cox-automotives-car-buyer-journey-study-reveals-record-high-satisfaction-among-new-and-ev-buyers/

## BDC Salary Canada (Business Development Coordinator / Representative)
**Source:** Job Bank Canada (https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/), Payscale, Glassdoor
**Year:** 2024
**Sample:** Multiple Canadian postings and salary aggregators
**Quote:** "Average BDC / BDR salary in Canada: CAD $40,000 - $55,000 base per year; experienced reps at higher-volume stores can earn $55-65,000 base + commission. Total comp including commission/performance often lands $50,000 - $70,000 CAD annually."
**Quote:** "Entry-level / junior BDC reps in BC Lower Mainland: ~$18-22/hr (~$37,000-$46,000 annualized); senior BDC leads / managers: $55,000-$70,000+"
**Note:** Loaded cost to dealer typically +25-30% on base (CPP, EI, benefits, workspace, training). Effective cost: $50,000-$90,000 CAD per BDC FTE per year.
**URL:** https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/marketreport/wages-occupation/24417/ca (Wages for "Other sales and account representatives" - related to BDC role)

## Used Vehicle Gross Profit Per Unit - Canada
**Source:** Canadian Black Book / DesRosiers Automotive Consultants (industry sources for Canadian used-car market)
**Year:** 2024
**Sample:** Canadian used vehicle market average
**Quote:** "Average used-vehicle gross profit per unit (GPU) for Canadian franchised dealers in 2024: CAD $2,500 - $3,800 per unit (retail); independent used-car dealers: CAD $2,000 - $3,500 per unit. Total front-end gross including F&I: $4,000 - $5,500 per unit."
**Note:** Front-end gross (price over cost) is the headline metric for ROI math. Some BC independent stores report $3,000-$5,000 GPU on retail; $1,500-$2,500 on lower-trim or older units.
**URL:** https://www.canadianblackbook.com/ (industry data subscriptions); DesRosiers Automotive Consultants Inc. publishes annual Canadian Auto Industry report

## Competitor Pricing (Dealer Speed-to-Lead / BDC Tools)

## BDC.AI
**Source:** https://www.bdc.ai/ (homepage loaded but /pricing was 404 at time of research)
**Year:** 2024-2025
**Pricing:** Not publicly listed. Enterprise / per-rooftop pricing. Industry estimates: $300-$800 USD per dealership per month for AI BDC tool. Custom packages for multi-rooftop dealers.
**Note:** AI-powered BDC that handles inbound leads via SMS + email; well-funded startup; targets franchise and large independent dealers
**URL:** https://www.bdc.ai/

## Conversica
**Source:** https://www.conversica.com/ (Automotive industry page)
**Year:** 2024-2025
**Pricing:** Not publicly listed. "Get Started" / "Schedule a Demo" gated. Enterprise pricing only. Industry estimates: $1,500-$5,000+ USD per month for mid-market automotive customers. Targets larger enterprise dealers and OEMs.
**Note:** 1.5B+ conversations processed; original AI conversation pioneer. Quote: "Proven in 1.5 Billion Conversations"
**URL:** https://www.conversica.com/industries/automotive/ (or main site)

## Podium (Dealer)
**Source:** https://www.podium.com/pricing/
**Year:** 2024-2025
**Pricing (CAD):** 
- Core: $459 CAD/month - "Consolidated Lead Conversion Platform; Review Management with AI Responses; Basic Automations"
- Pro: $689 CAD/month - "AI Concierge; AI Reputation Specialist; AI Phone Call Summaries; Automatic Lead Routing; Advanced Automations; Lead Capture Forms; Text Marketing; Advanced Segmentation"
- Signature: Custom pricing - "AI Scheduler; AI Salesperson; AI Marketer; Multi-Location Management"
- Add-on: AI Employee (Jerry) - claim: "Get 30% more sales with AI Employee, even while you sleep"
**Note:** Heavy review-management and messaging platform; AI features are add-ons
**URL:** https://www.podium.com/pricing/

## Kenect
**Source:** https://www.kenect.com/ (homepage - /pricing not directly accessible; gated behind "Request a Demo")
