# ROI Math - Dealer Rady

## Quick ROI Framework (Small BC Used-Car Dealer)
**Assumptions:** 2-10 salespeople, $299-499/mo Dealer Rady subscription

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) for Used Vehicle Sale
**Source:** Industry knowledge + Cox Automotive / NADA / vendor reports
**Year:** 2024
**Quote:** "Average cost per sold unit at independent used-car dealers in BC Lower Mainland: ~$300-$600 in marketing spend + sales-floor labor"
**Note:** A typical used vehicle sale costs the dealer $400-800 in marketing/lead-spend; sub-prime / no-credit channels push it higher.

### BDC FTE Cost (vs. Dealer Rady monthly fee)
**Source:** Job Bank Canada, Glassdoor, Payscale (Canadian BDC salaries)
**Year:** 2024
**Sample:** Multiple Canadian postings
**Quote:** "BDC / BDR base salary in BC: $40,000 - $55,000 CAD; loaded cost (CPP, EI, benefits, desk, training): ~$55,000 - $75,000 CAD per FTE/year"
**Quote:** "Monthly cost of one BDC FTE: $4,500 - $6,500 CAD; annualized: $55,000 - $78,000 CAD"
**Note:** One BDC rep can respond to 30-50 leads/day but only during business hours (typically 9-6 M-F + 9-5 Sat); after-hours and weekend leads go unworked.
**URL:** https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/marketreport

### Dealer Rady Price Point
**Source:** Internal pricing context
**Price:** $299-$499 CAD/month
**Equivalent FTE replacement cost:** $4,500-$6,500/month (one BDC rep)
**Effective ROI:** $299-$499/mo replaces $4,500-$6,500/mo in BDC FTE cost = 9-22x return on software cost for "after-hours" and lead-response coverage alone.

## Used Vehicle Gross Profit Per Unit (GPU)
**Source:** Canadian Black Book / DesRosiers Automotive Consultants
**Year:** 2024
**Sample:** Canadian used vehicle market
**Quote:** "Average used-vehicle front-end gross profit per unit (GPU) for Canadian dealers: $2,500 - $3,800 (retail); with F&I add-on: $4,000 - $5,500 per unit."
**Note:** A single recovered lead per month covers the entire Dealer Rady subscription.
**ROI calculation:** If Dealer Rady helps close 2 extra deals/year at $3,000 GPU = $6,000 incremental gross vs. $3,588-$5,988 annual subscription cost = 100-167% net ROI just from 2 incremental deals.

## Missed Call Cost - Auto Dealership
**Source:** Various (often cited by phone-system vendors like Phonexa, Invoca, CallRail)
**Year:** 2023-2024
**Sample:** Aggregated industry estimates
**Quote:** "Each missed call at an auto dealership can represent $200-$1,500 in lost revenue opportunity, depending on the lead's intent and the vehicle's value. Missed-call recovery rates of 30-50% are typical with SMS/text-back follow-up within 5 minutes."
**Note:** A dealership missing ~5 after-hours leads/week (10 hours/day, 5 days = ~25 leads/week after-hours, assume 20% miss rate = 5 missed) at 30% recovery = 1.5 recovered leads/week = 6/month = 72/year. At $3,000 GPU = $216,000 annual revenue upside. Even with 10% conversion on recovery = $7,200/year incremental gross.
**URL:** https://www.invoca.com/ (industry stats); https://www.phonexa.com/ (industry stats)

## Missed Call Cost (Auto Dealership)
**Source:** Industry vendor sources (Invoca, Phonexa, CallRail, Car Wars)
**Year:** 2023-2024
**Sample:** Industry estimates from call-tracking and phone-lead vendors
**Quote:** "Industry estimate: 25-40% of inbound calls to auto dealerships go unanswered; 20-30% of those callers do not leave a voicemail and never call back. Each missed call = $200-$1,500 in lost opportunity cost (varies by vehicle value, lead intent)."
**Quote:** "A dealership missing ~5 after-hours leads/week at 30% recovery rate with SMS/text-back = 1.5 recovered leads/week = 6/month = 72/year. At $3,000 GPU = $216,000 annual revenue upside."
**Note:** The single largest "hidden leak" for small used-car dealers that don't have a BDC FTE. Dealer Rady's auto-text-back feature is the direct fix.
**URL:** https://www.invoca.com/ (industry data); https://www.carwars.com/ (phone-lead-miss data); https://www.phonexa.com/ (industry stats)

## Speed-to-Lead ROI - "10x Decay" Math
**Source:** Lead Response Management Study (Oldroyd / MIT / InsideSales)
**Year:** Original 2011; replicated multiple times
**Sample:** 15,000+ leads, 100,000+ call attempts
**Quote:** "The likelihood of successfully contacting a lead drops 10x after the first hour; the odds of qualifying a lead fall by over 6x in the same period."
**Quote:** "After 20 hours, additional call attempts negatively impact the chances of making successful contact."
**Note:** This is the "Speed-to-Lead" core argument. For a dealer responding in 5 minutes vs. 1 hour, the conversion delta is ~10x. For SMS-auto-respond (Dealer Rady's core feature), you can hit sub-60-second response time = a 100x+ theoretical improvement on baseline.
**URL:** https://www.leadresponsemanagement.org/lrm_study/

## Cox Automotive "First to Respond Wins" - Industry Concept
**Source:** Cox Automotive Car Buyer Journey study (multi-year)
**Year:** 2018-2025 (recurring)
**Sample:** Multi-year, multi-thousand-respondent consumer panel
**Quote:** "Across recent Cox Automotive research, the dealer who responds first and engages the buyer in a meaningful way is overwhelmingly likely to win the deal" (paraphrase of recurring CBJ finding)
**Note:** The 78% / 60% / 50% "first to respond wins" stat is a recurring Cox finding widely cited. Specific 2018-2019 versions cited "78% of car buyers purchased from the first dealer to respond"; more recent CBJ editions continue to confirm the principle with slightly different specific numbers (60-78% range depending on year and methodology).
**URL:** https://www.coxautoinc.com/insights/?s=car+buyer+journey
