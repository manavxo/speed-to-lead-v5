The Work We Do

The Difference We Make

Built on Data to Put Dealers Back in Control

Lotlinx started with a simple truth: Dealers deserved more control over their inventory — and their success.

Our founders Len Short, Robert Vucic and Jason Knight brought decades of automotive experience together, along with a clear view of the industry’s blind spot. Dealers didn’t have the visibility to know which cars were at risk and why, or the tools to do anything about it. 

So, they built what was missing: a platform that puts dealers back in control. 

Leveraging the industry’s most robust dataset and machine learning, Lotlinx pioneered the first VIN-level approach to inventory management — helping dealers spot risk early, understand what’s holding cars back and take action to drive faster, more profitable sales.

That was 2012 — long before AI became the buzzword it is today. Even back then, our founders knew what others didn’t: it all comes down to the data. The data is the differentiator. 

Len was talking about predictive modeling and VIN-level optimization before the industry even had a name for it. Some thought he was crazy. The world wasn’t ready for it yet, but he was.

And Lotlinx led the way.

More than a decade later, that same data-first philosophy powers the industry’s most advanced VIN Performance platform — one that continues to evolve, putting data and decision-making back where they belong: in the hands of dealers.

Today, Lotlinx partners with thousands of dealers nationwide, including hundreds of rooftops across the country’s top auto groups, as well as OEMs, co-ops and resellers.

What hasn’t changed is our belief that technology should never replace people; it should empower them. Our data and tools are built to support the humans doing the real work: at the desk, on the lot and in the showroom.

And we’re just getting started.

Meet the leaders who drive us forward

Len Short

Executive Chairman

Len Short serves as executive chairman at Lotlinx. A respected leader in business for decades, Short uses his strong background in digital media to drive the company’s strategy, branding and customer service. Prior to founding Lotlinx, Short was the founding chief marketing officer at (product)RED, executive vice president of brand marketing at AOL/TimeWarner and chief marketing officer for Charles Schwab & Co. He also held various positions at national agencies such as NW Ayer, Ross Roy and Digitas. For his involvement in the automotive community, Short has been recognized by The American Marketing Association, the Effie Awards, the DMA Echo Awards, the Francis Hatch Awards and the International Advertising Festival at Cannes.

Jason Knight

CEO

Jason Knight is the co-founder and vice chairman at Lotlinx, bringing more than 25 years of management experience in automotive, digital, consumer packaged goods and manufacturing to the team. Prior to co-founding Lotlinx in 2012, he served as an executive in the consumer products, adult beverages and manufacturing industries, and was a private equity operator who helped grow and sell investor-owned businesses. Knight has been recognized as an innovator in the field by CIO Review and Business Rockstars and has been featured in editorial content and events by CBT News, DrivingSales, Digital Dealer, and Kain Automotive. He serves on Lotlinx’s board of directors, and is emeritus director of the Oakland Zoological Foundation Board, GEQ Capital, Lactalis American Group, HMR Foods and Boston Reed.

Rob Vucic

Co-Founder

Rob currently serves as the co-founder of Lotlinx, bringing over 24 years of experience in software security engineering and system scalability to the team. He is a seasoned software architect and senior developer with extensive knowledge of internet security threats. Before Lotlinx, he held leadership roles as co-founder and VP of engineering for Chug, Systems Architect at TRUSTe, and Software Security Engineer at Microsoft. At Microsoft, he designed "First Detection Sensors" for internet threats, earning an FBI award. Additionally, Rob has designed and developed key systems, including a scalable privacy seal hosting system, the first Customer Enterprise Portal, and a Unified Data Layer.

Lance Schafer

General Manager

Lance Schafer joined the team in April of 2015 and serves as the General Manager for Lotlinx. He helps lead and direct the Product and Technology team in the creation of valuable products for the Automotive Digital Marketing industry. Combining his Digital Marketing and software development expertise, Lance helped introduce machine learning to Lotlinx’s massive ad network and co-developed a technology patent. Lance, who is based out of our Canada office, came to Lotlinx following a 20-year career managing investment opportunities in the technology and digital media spaces. Having an entrepreneurial spirit, Lance has been CEO and Co-Founder of many business ventures, including co-founding LemonFree.com – a comprehensive online automotive shopping and research marketplace. LemonFree,com went on to be acquired by LotLinx in 2015, thus bringing Lance to our team.
